Hero Company is introducing a new Bike in 2023 which is Hero-X-Pulse-200-BS6. You can select the color of your bike which you want. Powering the XPulse 200 BS6 is a 199.6cc, single-cylinder engine that now features an oil-cooled setup instead of the air-cooled layout on the BS4 model. The updated motor produces 17.8bhp of power at 8,500rpm and 16.45Nm of torque at 6,500rpm. The engine is mated to a five-speed gearbox. The motorcycle has a seat height of 823mm and a high ground clearance of 220mm. Hero offers the XPulse 200 BS6 in a single variant with fuel-injection. The carburettor version has been discontinued
